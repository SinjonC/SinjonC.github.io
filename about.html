About

Created during the great web collapse of 2021, WEB.NET was made with the intention of cataloging and archiving all forms of web. Since the tragic loss of most of web knowledge, we strive to one day re-amass a complete and total log of Webs, to be used for both preservation and recreational purposes.    

Here at WEB.NET, we strive to document all Webs. While the work may be hard, we believe our effort is worth it. After we finish logging all currently known forms of Web, we plan to devote future resources to further deepen our understanding on just how far the Web goes...
